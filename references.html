References

This is the references page. Here you will find some references and resources to help you plan for the reuse of your data.

Resources

  • Looking for a repository to share your data?
    • Re3Data.org is a global catalog of data repositories from different academic disciplines. You can search or browse the catalog to find a disciplinary or institutional repository that meets your needs.
    • Figshare is a freely available repository where anyone can upload and share their data, code, and supporting documentation.
    • Zenodo is another freely available repository for sharing your data, code and documentation.
  • Trying to decide on a license? Check out ChooseALicense.com put together by GitHub.
  • Looking to develop a more in-depth Data Reuse Plan? Try our exercise on how to plan for data reuse.
